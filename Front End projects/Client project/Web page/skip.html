Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our next day service

What is the cutoff time for next day delivery?

The cutoff time is 3PM. Orders placed before 3PM will be picked up the same day and delivered the next business day.

What areas do you serve for next day delivery?

We serve all major cities across Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Faisalabad, Peshawar, and more.

What happens if my package isn't delivered on time?

If we miss our delivery window, you'll receive a full refund of your shipping fees as part of our money-back guarantee.

What items can I send with next day delivery?

We accept most non-hazardous items up to 5kg. Restricted items include cash, jewelry, and illegal goods.

Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?

Our next day service operates on business days only. Packages shipped on Friday will be delivered Monday.
